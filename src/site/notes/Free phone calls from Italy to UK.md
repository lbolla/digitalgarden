Since today, I always used [Skype](http://www.skype.com) to call landline and mobile phones in the UK: it is much cheaper than any Italian telephone company. But from today, I\'m going with something else: [VoipWise](http://www.voipwise.com)and [VoipBuster](http://www.voipbuster.com). Here is a comparison of the rates to call the UK:

-   [Skype](http://www.skype.com/intl/en/prices/callrates/#listing-U)

    -   landline: 0.020 eur/min
    -   mobile: 0.236 eur/min

-   [VoipWise](http://www.voipwise.com/en/rates.html)

    -   landline: FREE
    -   mobile: 0.100 eur/min

-   [VoipBuster](http://www.voipbuster.com/en/rates.html)

    -   landline: 0.010 eur/min
    -   mobile: 0.180 eur/min

VoipWise is the winner!!! Now I can call UK for free! I wonder how long it will last...
