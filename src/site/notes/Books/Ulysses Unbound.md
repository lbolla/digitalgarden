# Why
As a companion for [[Books/Ulysses\|Ulysses]].
# What I liked
The book is very schematic. Each chapter has the same structure: a summary of the corresponding chapter in [[Books/Ulysses\|Ulysses]], an explanation of the literary style, which body part governs the narration. It's an excellent book to read along the main work.
# What I disliked
The book doesn't provide novel insights: all it says can be found on Wikipedia or other online websites. Nonetheless, I appreciate having all the information in one place.
