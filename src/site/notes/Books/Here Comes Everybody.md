# Why
As a companion for [[Books/Ulysses\|Ulysses]].
# What I liked
The book gives a unitary view of _A Portrait_, [[Books/Ulysses\|Ulysses]] and _Finnegans Wake_. It's beautifully written and quite short (which is a great quality). If one doesn't feel like reading Joyce, this book is excellent on its own right to get a sense of the complexity and depth of Joyce's works.
# What I disliked
Nothing really. The only observation is that this is an "introductory" companion, meaning that one probably needs [[Books/Ulysses Unbound\|other secondary sources]] to help with [[Books/Ulysses\|Ulysses]].
