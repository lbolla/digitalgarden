# Why
Marshall McLuhan's [[Books/Understanding Media\|Understanding Media]] tickled my interest for James Joyce: [Finnegans Wake](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Finnegans_Wake) was the author's favorite book. I read _FW_'s first lines and I thought immediately I needed [[Books/Here Comes Everybody\|some help]]. Antony Burgess introduction convinced me that I would probably enjoy _Ulysses_ better: I had read _The Dubliners_ before and I'm not really that interested in _A Portrait_. More over _Ulysses_ has this aura of being a difficult book and I didn't quite know what the story was about...
