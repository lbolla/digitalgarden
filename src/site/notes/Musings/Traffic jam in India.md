A friend of mine sent me [this](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UmR9YpHOYEE). It reminded me of my trip in India just 4 months ago...

These guys live at an amazing speed... They never sleep! And they don\'t even need [AIM](http://www.cs.utexas.edu/users/kdresner/aim/?p=video) (autonomous intersection management)!

The street is the most similar thing to a vein I can imagine. The city is the body. I\'ve always thought that megalopolis are super-human and this confirms my hypothesis. Read [this](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0465026567/103-7617702-4063042?v=glance&n=283155) if you like the subject.
